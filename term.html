COVID Safety Protocols

Face masks and proof of vaccination are no longer required when using our services in accordance with provincial and federal guidelines.

Identification

We require all passengers over the age of 18 to travel with Government Issued photo ID or passport.

Check-In

Please check in 15 minutes prior to your departure.

Payment Terms

You can pay in cash or via online using a valid VISA or Master Credit/Debit card when making online reservations. Please check with your carrier for specific terms and conditions.

Change and Cancellation Policy

Pink Bullet: To change or cancel a ticket for a full refund, a minimum of 24-hour notice is required before departure. Failing to provide notice makes the trip non-refundable.

No Shows

If you don't show up or fail to arrive on time, your ticket is forfeited. A new ticket will need to be purchased unless prior notification is given before the scheduled departure time.

Zero Tolerance Drug and Alcohol Policy

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding intoxication. Any intoxicated passengers will be denied service for the safety and comfort of others.

Travel Policies for PinkBullet SafeBus in Sri Lanka

1. Passenger Identification

All passengers above the age of 18 must carry a government-issued photo ID or passport for ticket verification.

2. Reservation and Payment

Tickets can be booked online or in person.

Only VISA or MasterCard debit/credit cards are accepted for online bookings.

Cash payments are accepted at physical ticket counters.

3. Cancellation and Refunds

Cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to departure for a full refund. Failure to provide notice results in a non-refundable ticket.

4. Baggage Policy

Passengers are allowed one piece of carry-on baggage and one personal item. Large luggage may require an additional fee based on size and weight.

5. Seat Assignments

Seats are allocated at the time of booking. Specific seat requests may not always be accommodated due to availability.

7. Service Animals and Pets

Service animals accompanying passengers with disabilities are allowed. Other pets are not permitted on board unless in special carriers and with prior permission.

8. Passenger Conduct

Passengers are required to maintain appropriate behavior and follow staff instructions. Smoking, littering, or disruptive behavior is strictly prohibited onboard.
